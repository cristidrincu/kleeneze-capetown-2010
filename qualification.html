QUALIFICATION CRITERIA

PART ONE: THE GUARANTEED REWARD

Period 1 to Period 12, 2009 inclusive

NEW EXECUTIVES & SALES PLAN PROGRESSION:

Move one position up the Sales Plan to a MINIMUM of Bronze Executive (up to and including Period 9, 2009 ONLY).


HOLD the new position for a further 2 periods, up to and including Period 12, 2009.

EXISTING EXECUTIVES:

HIT highest ever PIN LEVEL (as at close of 2008) for 3 periods (between Period 1 and Period 9, inclusive).


Demonstrate 15% year-on-year GROWTH (in comparison to the same period in 2008).

SEDs:

Hit FIVE WIDE status for 5 periods (between Period 1 and Period 9, inclusive).


Demonstrate positive year-on-year GROWTH (in comparison to the same period in 2008).

PREMIERs:

Hit PREMIER status for 3 periods (between Period 1 and Period 9).


Demonstrate positive year-on-year GROWTH (in comparison to the same period in 2008).

Period 1 to Period 12, 2009 inclusive

League tables will be based on percentage growth (over the same period in the previous year, or a set minimum base level to be announced at the end of Period 9, whichever is the greatest).

The Competition is open to all positions at GOLD & ABOVE.

Competition places are limited and the total number available will be announced after Period 9, 2009, dependant on the number of qualifiers during Periods 1 – 9, 2009.

Qualification will be subject to the usual company verifications.

The programme commences at the point of departure of the international flight and therefore domestic flights (except from Ireland & the Channel Islands) are not included.

In order to qualify, a new partner on a Kleeneze distributorship should have joined the business before the distributorship begins qualification.
